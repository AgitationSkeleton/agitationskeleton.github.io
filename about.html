About Us
REDchanit was founded in 2015 and continues to this day to provide fun, fast-paced variety gameplay and instant action spanning across the Team Fortress series, its many mods and its adjacent games such as Quake and Doom, with the explicit goals to never monetize anything on the server and to always allow for free speech and micspam.
On top of that, a sense of lighthearted fun and a tightknit community.
